    What Can We Help You With?

    Can I change my default language?

    Yes! You can change your default language in your profile settings. You can update your language from your settings menu. Click Settings from dropdown in the upper right corner, then select your desired language from the Language dropdown.

    Printing your Images

    While there is no option to print an image directly from RED CARD, after you've saved your creation, you can print it using your printer software or use an online or nearby printing service. Please be sure to check that your image resolution falls within the minimum and maximum resolution required by your printing provider to ensure your printed image will result in just the right quality and correct image size.

    Resizing Your Images

    Resizing your photos can reduce both the resolution and the file size. Our Resize tool is especially useful when you have large photos that you'd like to upload to the web. The Resize tool can be located in the Photo Editor section.

    Saving as a Project to Edit Later

    If you'd like to save your work but still want the option to make future edits, you can save it as a RED CARD Project file (BFD). This will keep all of your layers intact, and allow you to pick up exactly where you left off! You can save your image as a RED CARD Project file in the Photo Editor, Graphic Designer, and Collage Maker - all following the same steps.
